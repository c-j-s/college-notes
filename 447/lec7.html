Definition: A Sequence is increasing iff ` S_n <= S_(n+1) `

Definition: A Sequence `S_n` is decreasing iff ` S_n >= S_(n+1) `

Definition: A sequence is monotone iff it is either increasing or decreasing

Examples:

  • `S_n = 2` is increasing and decreasing
  • `S_n = 1 + 1/n` is decreasing

Theoren 10.2: A bounded monotone sequence converges

Suppore `{ S_n }` is increasing
